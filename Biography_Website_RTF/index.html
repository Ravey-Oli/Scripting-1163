Biograpghy

Raven Tailfeathers is a second-year student in Multimedia production at Lethbridge college, Born in Calgary Alberta, August 21st.

Identifying as two-spirited and using she/her pronouns she started her transition in 2018, weeks after her 18th birthday.

Home is the surrounding Blood Reservation. Raised by traditional Blackfoot parents incorporate a strong sense of family and culture. But growing up isolated and only around family.

Interests are varied with, media and technology being the most passionate interests. Art being the other interest, as visually expressing ideas is a skill she wishes to get better at.

Hobbies include watching movies and watching sports, as well as playing video games, listing to music, reading occasionally.

Graduating from Red Crow college in 2020, Attending a summer camp at the college and made the decision to apply to the Multimedia production program shortly thereafter.

Most recently Employed over the summer at Opokaa'sin as the Media/Technology assistant on a project. Prior to that working as a Bank Teller at TD bank as well as a teaching assistant. At Tatsikiisaapo'p Middle School.

Grand aspirations include pursuing a chance to work on a successful project, for larger companies in the future.

Kodiaks new Indigenous jerseys for the e-sports team (L), soccer (C), and cross country (R), September 29, 2023. (Credit:LNN)

Lethbridge College has unveiled new Indigenous jerseys for all of its Kodiaks Athletics teams. The jerseys feature the Aikowania Bear logo, designed by artist Monte Eagle Plume using traditional Blackfoot painting methods. Also on the jersey is a pattern resembling a Pendelton Blanket designed by Multimedia Production student Raven Tailfeathers a Sspomitapiiski (Star People) design on the arms, and a mountain design on the back.
