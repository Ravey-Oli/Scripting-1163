Enforcing Humanity's Boundaries

Welcome to the Los Angeles Police Department, Replicant Detection Unit (RPD), a specialized unit tasked with the complex responsibility of managing and monitoring advanced artificial intelligence and android technology in our ever-evolving world. In a society where humans and machines coexist, the Replicant Detection Unit plays a pivotal role in maintaining order, ethics, and safety.

Mission Statement

To create a world where humanity and technology coexist harmoniously, where the boundaries between the two are respected, and where every individual can live free from the fear of rogue replicants.

The Replicant Detection Unit, our mission is to safeguard humanity in an ever-evolving world where the line between human and artificial life blurs. We are entrusted with the responsibility to maintain order and security by identifying and neutralizing rogue replicants assets thereby preserving the fragile balance between man and machine.

Commitment to Community

The Replicant Detection Unit is committed to ensuring the safety and well-being of our community through its unwavering dedication to identifying and managing replicant-related threats. Our mission is to uphold the following core principles in service to the community

Public Safety

The (RDU) is dedicated to safeguarding the public from replicant-related risks. We employ cutting-edge technology, expertise, and investigative techniques to detect and neutralize potential threats promptly.

Transparency

We believe in transparency as the foundation of trust within our community. The (RDU) is committed to providing clear and open communication regarding our activities, procedures, and findings related to replicant detection.

Equal Protection

The (RDU) is devoted to treating all members of our community with fairness and impartiality. We ensure that replicant detection efforts are carried out without discrimination, respecting the rights and dignity of every individual.

Community Engagement

We actively engage with our community, seeking their input, concerns, and cooperation in our efforts. Public awareness and collaboration are vital in our mission to maintain a safe environment.

Education and Awareness

The (RDU) believes in the importance of educating the community about replicants and their potential risks. We provide resources and information to help individuals recognize and report replicant-related activities.

Prevention and Resolution

Our unit is proactive in preventing replicant-related incidents and addressing them swiftly when they occur. We work diligently to minimize potential harm and disruptions to the community.

Ethical Conduct

The (RDU) adheres to the highest ethical standards in all our operations. We prioritize the protection of human rights and ethical considerations when dealing with replicants.

Continuous Improvement

We are committed to ongoing training, research, and development to enhance our replicant detection capabilities. Continuous improvement ensures that we remain effective in addressing emerging challenges.

Accountability

The (RDU) holds itself accountable for its actions and decisions. We are responsive to community feedback and take corrective measures when necessary.

Public Trust

We understand that public trust is our most valuable asset. The (RDU) works tirelessly to maintain the confidence and support of our community, ensuring that they feel safe and secure.

Unit History

In 2009, Officer Cal Moreaux, a Detective with the Los Angeles Police Department, investigated a series of cases involving replicants. His search began with a break-in by one such replicant, followed by a suspicious suicide case within the Tyrell Corporation.

As Officer Moreaux uncovered the mysteries, he found himself in a search surrounding the Nexus-5 replicants. Joining forces with a replicant, Detective Moreaux embarked on a mission to uncover the truth.

Their efforts led to the rescue of an individual named Marcus and unveiled the unsettling reality behind the Nexus-5 replicants. During their pursuit of a rogue Tyrell Corporation representative, a tragic incident occurred involving a person known to Moreaux.

In a dramatic turn of events, Detective Moreaux's own sister, now transformed into a Nexus-5 replicant, hatched a plan to expose the humanity within their kind. In a heart-wrenching act, Detective Moreaux fulfilled her final wish.

Detective Moreaux then took a pivotal role in the creation of the Replicant Detection Unit within the LAPD, this is the history of Detective Cal Moreaux's tireless pursuit of justice in the world of replicants.
