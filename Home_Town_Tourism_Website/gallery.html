Polson Cherry Festival

The locality is renowned for producing cherries and has numerous cherry orchards in the vicinity. Every year, the downtown Cherry Festival is held in Polson in celebration of the abundant harvest.

Ronan Powwow

The Ronan Powwow is an event that takes place in the month of August and showcases the customs, rituals, and traditions of local Native Americans

Attendees can witness traditional dances and experience rhythmic drumming that celebrates Native American culture. The event also offers unique pieces of art and crafts for visitors to appreciate. Additionally, attendees can sample authentic Native American cuisine.
