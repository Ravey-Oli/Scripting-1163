Flathead Lake

This majestic lake is the ultimate destination for those who crave a one-of-a-kind outdoor experience, thanks to its abundant recreational opportunities.

To add to its allure, the vast expanse of this lake surpasses that of other natural freshwater lakes in the Western United States, opening endless aquatic adventures such as swimming, boating, fishing and picnicking that are hard to find elsewhere.

Polson Bay Golf Course

An exceptional golf course that provides a memorable experience for all golf enthusiasts, whether beginner or advanced.

The picturesque landscape offers a serene view of the Flathead Lake and the grandiose Mission Mountains, creating an ambiance of pure bliss. It is an excellent place to relax, rejuvenate and enjoy a perfect day of golfing.
